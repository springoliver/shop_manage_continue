Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their account and associated data for our application, in accordance with Google Play and Google Developer policies.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, please follow one of the methods below:

  • Send an email request from the email address associated with your account.
  • Include your registered email or user ID in the message.
Contact Email:

support@maximanage.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Authentication credentials
  • App-generated content linked to your account

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law, regulatory obligations, or for fraud prevention. Any retained data will be securely stored and automatically deleted once the retention period ends.
